About Us

Who We Are

At E-Waste Crisis, we are passionate about creating a sustainable future by tackling one of the fastest-growing environmental threats—electronic waste.

Our Mission

We aim to raise awareness about the e-waste crisis and provide an easy, accessible, and tech-driven approach to recycling. By integrating AI technology and gamification, we make recycling electronic waste engaging and impactful.

How We Help

  • 🚀 AI-Powered E-Waste Scanner – Identify and classify e-waste instantly.
  • ♻ Find Nearby Recycling Centers – Locate authorized e-waste disposal sites near you.
  • 🏆 Gamification & Rewards – Earn points and rewards for responsible recycling.
  • 🌱 Sustainable Future – Join us in reducing e-waste.
